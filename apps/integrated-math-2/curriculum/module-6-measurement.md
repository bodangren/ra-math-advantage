# Module 6: Measurement

## Overview
This module develops formulas and techniques for measuring two-dimensional areas and three-dimensional surface areas and volumes. Students apply area formulas to regular polygons, calculate surface area and volume of prisms, pyramids, cylinders, cones, and spheres, and use similarity to solve density and composite solid problems.

## Skills Developed
- Calculate areas of quadrilaterals including parallelograms, trapezoids, and rhombi
- Find areas of regular polygons using apothem and side length
- Compute areas of circles, sectors, and segments
- Calculate surface area of three-dimensional solids using nets and formulas
- Identify cross-sections of solids and solids of revolution
- Apply volume formulas for prisms, pyramids, cylinders, cones, and spheres
- Use similarity to relate surface areas and volumes of solids
- Solve density problems by relating mass, volume, and population density
