# Unit 5: Circles

## Overview

Students develop understanding of circle vocabulary, angle and arc relationships, chord properties, tangent and secant lines, and the equation of a circle in the coordinate plane.

## Lessons

### 5-1 Circle Vocabulary and Basic Properties

Parts of a circle (radius, diameter, chord, secant, tangent, arc, sector, central angle), arc length, and sector area.

### 5-2 Central Angles and Inscribed Angles

The inscribed angle theorem (inscribed angle = 1/2 intercepted arc), angles in a semicircle, and applications.

### 5-3 Arcs and Chords

Congruent chords, perpendicular from center bisects chord, and arc-chord relationships.

### 5-4 Tangent and Secant Lines

Tangent perpendicular to radius, tangent-tangent and secant-secant relationships, and power of a point.

### 5-5 Equations of Circles

Standard form $(x - h)^2 + (y - k)^2 = r^2$, completing the square, and graphing circles.

## Skills Developed

- Identifying and naming parts of a circle
- Applying the inscribed angle theorem
- Using arc and chord relationships to solve problems
- Analyzing tangent and secant segment relationships
- Writing and graphing equations of circles
- Connecting circle geometry to coordinate algebra
