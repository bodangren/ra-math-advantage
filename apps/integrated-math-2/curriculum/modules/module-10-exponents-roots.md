# Unit 10: Exponents and Roots

## Overview

Students extend their understanding of exponents to include negative and rational exponents, apply laws of exponents, work with scientific notation, simplify radical expressions, and solve radical equations.

## Lessons

### 10-1 Laws of Exponents

Apply the product rule, quotient rule, and power rule to simplify exponential expressions. Combine multiple rules in complex expressions.

### 10-2 Negative and Rational Exponents

Rewrite expressions with negative exponents using positive exponents. Evaluate and simplify expressions with rational exponents. Convert between radical and exponential form.

### 10-3 Scientific Notation

Convert between standard form and scientific notation. Multiply, divide, add, and subtract numbers in scientific notation. Apply to real-world measurements.

### 10-4 Radical Expressions

Simplify square roots and cube roots. Add, subtract, multiply radical expressions. Rationalize denominators. Simplify radicals with variables.

### 10-5 Solving Radical Equations

Isolate the radical and square both sides. Check for extraneous solutions. Solve equations with cube roots. Apply to real-world problems.

## Skills Developed

- Applying product, quotient, and power rules for exponents
- Simplifying expressions with negative and rational exponents
- Performing operations in scientific notation
- Simplifying and operating with radical expressions
- Solving radical equations and checking for extraneous solutions
