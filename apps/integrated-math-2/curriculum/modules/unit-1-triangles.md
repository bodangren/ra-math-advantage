# Unit 1: Relationships in Triangles

## Overview

Students develop understanding of triangle properties, congruence, and reasoning through proof and construction.

## Lessons

### 1-1 Classifying Triangles

Classification by sides (scalene, isosceles, equilateral) and angles (acute, right, obtuse, equiangular).

### 1-2 Triangle Angle Relationships

Triangle angle sum theorem, exterior angle theorem, and relationships between angles and sides.

### 1-3 Triangle Inequality Theorem

Determining whether three lengths can form a triangle; ordering sides and angles.

### 1-4 Congruence Criteria (SSS, SAS, ASA, AAS, HL)

Identifying and applying triangle congruence shortcuts.

### 1-5 Proving Triangle Congruence

Writing two-column proofs using congruence criteria.

### 1-6 Isosceles & Equilateral Triangle Properties

Base angles, symmetry, and special properties of isosceles and equilateral triangles.

## Skills Developed

- Classification and categorization of geometric figures
- Logical reasoning and proof construction
- Application of congruence criteria to real-world contexts
