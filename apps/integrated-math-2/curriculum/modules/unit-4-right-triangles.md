# Unit 4: Right Triangles & Trigonometry

## Overview

Students develop understanding of right triangle relationships, the Pythagorean theorem, special right triangles, trigonometric ratios, and the Law of Sines and Cosines for solving any triangle.

## Lessons

### 4-1 Pythagorean Theorem

The theorem and its converse, Pythagorean triples, and the distance formula derived from the Pythagorean theorem.

### 4-2 Special Right Triangles (30-60-90, 45-45-90)

Side ratios in 30-60-90 and 45-45-90 triangles, and applying these ratios to find missing sides.

### 4-3 Introduction to Sine, Cosine, Tangent

Defining the three primary trigonometric ratios, using them to find sides and angles in right triangles.

### 4-4 Solving Right Triangles

Finding all missing sides and angles in right triangles using trigonometric ratios and inverse functions.

### 4-5 Law of Sines

Extending trigonometry to oblique triangles, the ambiguous case (SSA), and area using (1/2)ab·sin(C).

### 4-6 Law of Cosines

Solving triangles with SAS and SSS information, Heron's formula for area, choosing the right tool.

## Skills Developed

- Applying the Pythagorean theorem and its converse
- Using special right triangle ratios for efficient computation
- Computing and applying trigonometric ratios
- Solving oblique triangles with Law of Sines and Law of Cosines
- Real-world measurement and navigation problems
