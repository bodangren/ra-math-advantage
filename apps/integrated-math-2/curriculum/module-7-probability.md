# Module 7: Probability

## Overview
This module develops counting techniques including permutations and combinations, and applies these to probability calculations. Students explore sample spaces, compound events, conditional probability, and two-way frequency tables, using probability to make informed decisions and predictions.

## Lessons
### 7-1 Sample Spaces
### 7-2 Probability and Counting
### 7-3 Geometric Probability
### 7-4 Probability with Permutations and Combinations
### 7-5 Probability and the Multiplication Rule
### 7-6 Probability and the Addition Rule
### 7-7 Conditional Probability
### 7-8 Two-Way Frequency Tables

## Skills Developed
- Represent sample spaces using organized lists, tables, and tree diagrams
- Apply the Fundamental Counting Principle to determine total outcomes
- Calculate permutations and combinations for arrangement and selection problems
- Find probabilities of independent and dependent compound events
- Apply the multiplication rule for conditional probability situations
- Use the addition rule and inclusion-exclusion for probability of unions
- Calculate and interpret conditional probability using formulas and two-way tables
- Use geometric probability to find likelihood of spatial outcomes
