# Module 10: Exponents and Roots

## Overview
Students build fluency with exponent properties, extending from multiplication to division, negative exponents, and rational exponents. They connect radical notation to exponential form, simplify radical expressions, and solve exponential equations using common bases.

## Lessons
### 10-1 Multiplication Properties of Exponents
### 10-2 Division Properties of Exponents
### 10-3 Negative Exponents
### 10-4 Rational Exponents
### 10-5 Simplifying Radical Expressions
### 10-6 Operations with Radical Expressions
### 10-7 Exponential Equations

## Skills Developed
- Applying product and quotient of powers properties
- Rewriting expressions with negative exponents in positive form
- Converting between radical and rational exponent notation
- Simplifying radical expressions using product and quotient properties
- Adding, subtracting, and multiplying radical expressions
- Solving exponential equations using common bases
