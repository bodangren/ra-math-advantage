# Module 1: Relationships in Triangles

## Overview
This module explores the properties and relationships within triangles, including perpendicular bisectors, angle bisectors, medians, and altitudes. Students develop geometric reasoning skills through constructions, proofs, and inequality relationships that build a foundation for more advanced geometric concepts.

## Lessons
### 1-1 Perpendicular Bisectors
### 1-2 Angle Bisectors
### 1-3 Medians and Altitudes of Triangles
### 1-4 Inequalities in One Triangle
### 1-5 Indirect Proof
### 1-6 The Triangle Inequality
### 1-7 Inequalities in Two Triangles

## Skills Developed
- Construct perpendicular bisectors and angle bisectors using geometric tools
- Identify and apply properties of medians and altitudes in triangles
- Prove triangle relationships using indirect proof techniques
- Apply the triangle inequality theorem to determine possible triangle side lengths
- Compare angles and sides using inequality relationships in triangles
- Write geometric proofs involving triangle centers and inequalities
