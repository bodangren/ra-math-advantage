# Module 2: Quadrilaterals

## Overview
This module focuses on the classification, properties, and proofs related to quadrilaterals including parallelograms, rectangles, rhombi, squares, trapezoids, and kites. Students learn to identify parallelogram types using coordinate geometry and apply properties to solve real-world problems involving two-dimensional shapes.

## Lessons
### 2-1 Angles of Polygons
### 2-2 Parallelograms
### 2-3 Tests for Parallelograms
### 2-4 Rectangles
### 2-5 Rhombi and Squares
### 2-6 Trapezoids and Kites

## Skills Developed
- Apply the polygon angle sum theorem to find interior and exterior angles
- Prove and use properties of parallelograms in geometric proofs
- Test for parallelogram conditions using coordinate geometry
- Distinguish between rectangles, rhombi, and squares and their unique properties
- Solve problems involving trapezoid midsegments and base angles
- Use slope, distance, and midpoint formulas for coordinate proofs
