# Module 3: Similarity

## Overview
This module introduces similarity transformations through dilations and develops criteria for triangle similarity (AA, SSS, SAS). Students apply proportional reasoning to solve problems involving indirect measurement, similar polygons, and triangle proportionality, establishing foundations for trigonometry and scaling applications.

## Lessons
### 3-1 Dilations
### 3-2 Similar Polygons
### 3-3 Similar Triangles: AA Similarity
### 3-4 Similar Triangles: SSS and SAS Similarity
### 3-5 Triangle Proportionality
### 3-6 Parts of Similar Triangles

## Skills Developed
- Perform and describe dilations with given scale factors
- Identify corresponding parts of similar figures
- Apply AA, SSS, and SAS similarity criteria to prove triangles similar
- Use triangle proportionality theorems to find unknown lengths
- Apply similarity to solve indirect measurement problems
- Find relationships between perimeters, areas, and altitudes of similar triangles
