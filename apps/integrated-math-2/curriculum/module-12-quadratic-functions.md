# Module 12: Quadratic Functions

## Overview
Students explore quadratic functions in depth — graphing in vertex form, factoring and solving quadratic equations, completing the square, using the quadratic formula, and applying quadratic models to real-world problems involving projectile motion, optimization, and area.

## Lessons
### 12-1 Graphing Quadratics in Vertex Form
### 12-2 Factoring Quadratics
### 12-3 Completing the Square
### 12-4 Quadratic Formula and the Discriminant
### 12-5 Applications of Quadratic Models

## Skills Developed
- Graphing quadratic functions and identifying key features
- Factoring and solving quadratic equations
- Completing the square to convert between forms
- Applying the quadratic formula and interpreting the discriminant
- Modeling and solving real-world problems with quadratics
