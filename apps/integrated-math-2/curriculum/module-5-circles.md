# Module 5: Circles

## Overview
This module covers circle vocabulary, arc and angle relationships, chord properties, and tangent-secant theorems. Students write equations of circles in the coordinate plane and explore parabolas, developing skills needed for analytic geometry and conic sections in later courses.

### 5-7 Equations of Circles
### 5-8 Equations of Parabolas

## Skills Developed
- Identify and define parts of a circle including radius, diameter, chord, secant, and tangent
- Find arc measures and differentiate between minor arcs, major arcs, and semicircles
- Apply theorems relating chords, arcs, and central angles
- Use inscribed angle theorems to find unknown angle measures
- Apply tangent and secant theorems including the power of a point
- Write and graph equations of circles in standard form
- Derive and use equations of parabolas from geometric definitions
