# Module 4: Right Triangles and Trigonometry

## Overview
This module extends right triangle relationships through the Pythagorean theorem, special right triangle ratios, and trigonometric ratios. Students solve triangles using sine, cosine, and tangent, then extend their toolkit with the Law of Sines and Law of Cosines to solve any triangle for real-world and mathematical applications.

## Skills Developed
- Use geometric mean to solve proportion problems involving right triangles
- Apply and prove the Pythagorean theorem and its converse
- Solve problems involving 30-60-90 and 45-45-90 triangle ratios
- Find sides and angles in right triangles using sine, cosine, and tangent
- Apply trigonometry to solve real-world measurement problems
- Use the Law of Sines to solve oblique triangles with ambiguous cases
- Use the Law of Cosines to solve triangles when SAS or SSS information is known
