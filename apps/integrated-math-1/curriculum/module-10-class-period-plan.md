## Notes

- This is a **planning model**, not a literal calendar.
- Most instruction days use **2-3 worked examples**.
- Module 10 (Tools of Geometry) covers foundational geometry vocabulary and measurement skills: axiomatic systems, points/lines/planes, segment measures, distance formula, partitioning, and midpoints.
- Lessons 10-1 through 10-3 establish foundational vocabulary and measurement; Lessons 10-4 through 10-7 focus on coordinate-based measurement and formulas.

## Why This Split Works

- `10-1` is foundational vocabulary ‚Äî two days to establish the axiomatic mindset and distinguish synthetic from analytic geometry.
- `10-2` (points, lines, planes) is vocabulary-heavy and benefits from both identification and construction examples.
- `10-3` (segment measures) splits naturally between number line reading and the Segment Addition Postulate with algebra.
- `10-4` (distance) covers both number line and coordinate plane distance ‚Äî a denser lesson that benefits from two days.
- `10-5` and `10-6` (partitioning) form a coherent sequence from number line to coordinate plane.
- `10-7` (midpoints) extends partitioning to finding missing endpoints ‚Äî a natural continuation.
- The non-instruction days support mastery of foundational measurement skills before testing.
