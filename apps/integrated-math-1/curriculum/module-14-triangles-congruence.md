# Module 14: Triangles and Congruence

## Overview
This module focuses on triangle properties, classification, and proof. Students apply the Triangle Angle-Sum Theorem and Exterior Angle Theorem, prove triangles congruent using SSS, SAS, ASA, AAS, and special right triangle congruence theorems (LL, HL, HA, LA), and use the Isosceles Triangle Theorem and its converse. The module concludes with coordinate proofs, where students place triangles on the coordinate plane and use algebraic methods to verify geometric properties.

## Skills Developed
- Apply the Triangle Angle-Sum Theorem and Exterior Angle Theorem
- Classify triangles as acute, right, or obtuse based on angle measures
- Write congruence statements and identify corresponding parts
- Use SSS, SAS, ASA, and AAS to prove triangle congruence
- Apply right triangle congruence theorems (LL, HL, HA, LA)
- Use the Distance Formula on the coordinate plane to verify SSS
- Apply the Isosceles Triangle Theorem and its converse
- Use the Reflexive Property and properties of midpoints and angle bisectors in proofs
- Position triangles strategically on the coordinate plane for coordinate proofs
- Use distance, slope, and midpoint formulas in coordinate proofs
- Classify triangles by their side lengths and angle measures using coordinate methods
