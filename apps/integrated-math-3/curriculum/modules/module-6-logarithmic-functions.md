# Module 6: Logarithmic Functions

## Overview

Students formalize logarithms as inverses of exponentials and apply them to solve complex equations.

## Lessons

### 6-1 Logarithms and Logarithmic Functions
Definition, graphs, and transformations.

### 6-2 Properties of Logarithms
Product, quotient, and power rules.

### 6-3 Common Logarithms
Base-10 applications.

### 6-4 Natural Logarithms
Base e and applications.

### 6-5 Using Exponential and Logarithmic Functions
Solving real-world problems.

## Skills Developed

- Rewriting expressions flexibly
- Solving non-linear equations
- Interpreting logarithmic scales
