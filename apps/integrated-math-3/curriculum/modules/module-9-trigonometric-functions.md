# Module 9: Trigonometric Functions

## Overview

Students explore periodic phenomena and trigonometric relationships using the unit circle.

## Lessons

### 9-1 Angles and Angle Measure
Degrees, radians, and arc length.

### 9-2 Trigonometric Functions of General Angles
Extending ratios beyond right triangles.

### 9-3 Circular and Periodic Functions
Unit circle and special angles.

### 9-4 Graphing Sine and Cosine Functions
Amplitude, period, and midline.

### 9-5 Graphing Other Trigonometric Functions
Tangent and reciprocal functions.

### 9-6 Translations of Trigonometric Graphs
Phase shift and vertical shifts.

### 9-7 Inverse Trigonometric Functions
Restricting domains and solving equations.

## Skills Developed

- Modeling periodic behavior
- Connecting algebra and geometry
- Interpreting transformations
