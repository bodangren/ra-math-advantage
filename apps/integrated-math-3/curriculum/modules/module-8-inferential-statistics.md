# Module 8: Inferential Statistics

## Overview

Students use data and probability to draw conclusions about populations.

## Lessons

### 8-1 Random Sampling
Bias and representative samples.

### 8-2 Using Statistical Experiments
Simulations and experimental design.

### 8-3 Analyzing Population Data
Measures of center and spread.

### 8-4 Normal Distributions
Bell curve, z-scores, and probabilities.

### 8-5 Estimating Population Parameters
Confidence intervals and inference.

## Skills Developed

- Evaluating data reliability
- Interpreting distributions
- Making evidence-based conclusions
