# Module 2: Polynomials and Polynomial Functions

## Overview

Students extend quadratic ideas to higher-degree polynomials, focusing on structure, behavior, and operations.

## Lessons

### 2-1 Polynomial Functions
Definitions, degree, leading coefficient, and basic graph shapes.

### 2-2 Analyzing Graphs of Polynomial Functions
End behavior, turning points, and multiplicity of roots.

### 2-3 Operations with Polynomials
Addition, subtraction, and multiplication (including distributive structures).

### 2-4 Dividing Polynomials
Long division and synthetic division; interpreting remainders.

### 2-5 Powers of Binomials
Pascal's Triangle and Binomial Theorem (conceptual introduction).

## Skills Developed

- Structural analysis of functions
- Fluency in symbolic manipulation
- Pattern recognition
