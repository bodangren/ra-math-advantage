# Unit 3: Trigonometric and Polar Functions

## Overview

This unit covers trigonometric and polar functions, including periodic phenomena, the unit circle, sinusoidal functions, tangent and reciprocal functions, inverse trig functions, polar coordinates, and rates of change in polar functions.

## AP Exam Status

- Assessed on AP Exam
- Multiple-choice weighting: 30-35%
- Suggested pacing: 7-10 weeks
