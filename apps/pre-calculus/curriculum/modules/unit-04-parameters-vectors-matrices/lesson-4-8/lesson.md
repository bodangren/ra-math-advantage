# Lesson 4-8 — Vectors

Unit: 4
Topic: 4.8

## Explore

### Section: text

A hiker walks 3 miles east and 4 miles north. How far is she from the starting point? The direct distance is 5 miles — but the *displacement* is more than a number. It has both magnitude (5 miles) and direction (northeast). A **vector** captures both of these quantities in a single mathematical object.

## Vocabulary

### Section: text

- **Vector**: A quantity with both magnitude and direction, written as [langle a, brangle] (component form).
- **Component**: The individual entries [a] and [b] of [langle a, brangle]. [a] is the horizontal component; [b] is the vertical.
- **Magnitude**: The length of the vector: [|langle a, brangle| = sqrt{a^2 + b^2}].
- **Unit vector**: A vector with magnitude 1. The standard unit vectors are [mathbf{i} = langle 1, 0rangle] and [mathbf{j} = langle 0, 1rangle].
- **Direction angle**: The angle [theta] the vector makes with the positive [x]-axis: [theta = arctanleft(frac{b}{a}right)].

## Learn

### Section: text

A **vector** [mathbf{v} = langle a, brangle] has two components. Its **magnitude** is:

\[ |mathbf{v}| = sqrt{a^2 + b^2} \]

**Operations**:
- **Addition**: [langle a_1, b_1rangle + langle a_2, b_2rangle = langle a_1 + a_2, b_1 + b_2rangle]
- **Scalar multiplication**: [c langle a, brangle = langle ca, cbrangle]
- **Subtraction**: [mathbf{u} - mathbf{v} = mathbf{u} + (-mathbf{v})]

**Geometric meaning**: Addition corresponds to placing vectors tip-to-tail. Scalar multiplication stretches or reverses the vector.

**Unit vector in the direction of [mathbf{v}]**: [frac{mathbf{v}}{|mathbf{v}|}] — same direction, magnitude 1.

## Worked Example

### Section: text

**Example 1:** Given [mathbf{u} = langle 3, -4rangle] and [mathbf{v} = langle -1, 7rangle]:

**(a)** Find [mathbf{u} + mathbf{v}]:
\[ langle 3 + (-1), -4 + 7rangle = langle 2, 3rangle \]

**(b)** Find [|mathbf{u}|]:
\[ sqrt{3^2 + (-4)^2} = sqrt{9 + 16} = sqrt{25} = 5 \]

**(c)** Find the unit vector in the direction of [mathbf{v}]:
\[ |mathbf{v}| = sqrt{(-1)^2 + 7^2} = sqrt{50} = 5sqrt{2} \]
\[ frac{mathbf{v}}{|mathbf{v}|} = leftlangle frac{-1}{5sqrt{2}}, frac{7}{5sqrt{2}}rightrangle = leftlangle frac{-sqrt{2}}{10}, frac{7sqrt{2}}{10}rightrangle \]
