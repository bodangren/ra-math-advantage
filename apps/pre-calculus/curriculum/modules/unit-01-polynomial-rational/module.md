# Unit 1: Polynomial and Rational Functions

## Overview

This unit covers polynomial and rational functions, including their rates of change, zeros, end behavior, asymptotes, and transformations. Students learn to analyze function behavior graphically, analytically, and numerically.

## AP Exam Status

- Assessed on AP Exam
- Multiple-choice weighting: 30-40%
- Suggested pacing: 6-8 weeks
