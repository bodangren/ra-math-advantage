# Module 4: Inverses and Radical Functions

## Overview

Students explore inverse relationships and extend function understanding to radicals and rational exponents.

## Lessons

### 4-1 Operations on Functions
Addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, and composition.

### 4-2 Inverse Functions
Finding inverses algebraically and graphically; horizontal line test.

### 4-3 Rational Exponents and Roots
Connecting exponent rules with radical notation.

### 4-4 Graphing Radical Functions
Transformations and domain/range restrictions.

### 4-5 Operations with Radicals
Simplifying and combining radical expressions.

### 4-6 Solving Radical Equations
Isolating radicals and checking for extraneous solutions.

## Skills Developed

- Understanding inverse relationships
- Managing domain restrictions
- Translating between representations
