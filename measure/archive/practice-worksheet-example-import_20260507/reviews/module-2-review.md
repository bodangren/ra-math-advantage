## Lesson Progression

The module flows logically:

1. **2-1** Writing & Interpreting Equations — establishes the vocabulary and translation skills.
2. **2-2** One-Step Equations — introduces the Properties of Equality.
3. **2-3** Multi-Step Equations — builds complexity with two or more operations.
4. **2-4** Variable on Each Side — adds the challenge of collecting variable terms.
5. **2-5** Absolute Value — introduces a new equation type with special cases (no solution).
6. **2-6** Proportions — shifts to ratio/equivalence reasoning (Cross Products Property).
7. **2-7** Using Formulas — culminates in literal equations, constraints, and dimensional analysis.

This progression is pedagogically sound: it moves from simple to complex, from numeric to abstract, and introduces a variety of equation structures before applying them to formulas.
