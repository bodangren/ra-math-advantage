## Lesson Progression Assessment

The module follows a logical instructional sequence:

1. **Lesson 5-1** — Foundational skill: writing equations in slope-intercept form (students' primary familiar form).
2. **Lesson 5-2** — Expands to point-slope and standard forms; introduces parallel and perpendicular relationships.
3. **Lesson 5-3** — Transitions from exact equations to data analysis with scatter plots and lines of fit.
4. **Lesson 5-4** — Adds conceptual depth by distinguishing correlation from causation.
5. **Lesson 5-5** — Formalizes data analysis with technology-driven linear regression, residuals, and the correlation coefficient.
6. **Lesson 5-6** — Culminates with inverse functions, an advanced algebraic topic that builds on all prior equation-writing skills.

**Verdict:** Progression is coherent and pedagogically sound.
