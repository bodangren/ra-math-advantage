## Lesson Progression Assessment

**Result: LOGICAL AND WELL-STRUCTURED**

The module follows a coherent instructional arc:

1. **8-1 Exponential Functions** — Introduces the function family, common ratio, key features, and graphing.
2. **8-2 Transformations** — Builds on 8-1 by showing how parameters shift, stretch, and reflect exponential graphs.
3. **8-3 Writing Functions** — Teaches students to derive equations from points, graphs, and real-world contexts (including compound interest).
4. **8-4 Transforming Expressions** — Applies exponent properties to compare rates with different compounding periods; natural extension of 8-3's compound interest coverage.
5. **8-5 Geometric Sequences** — Introduces the discrete counterpart to exponential functions, reinforcing common ratio and explicit formulas.
6. **8-6 Recursive Formulas** — Provides an alternative representation for sequences, building directly on geometric sequences from 8-5 and connecting back to arithmetic sequences from earlier modules.

The flow moves smoothly from **concept introduction → graphical manipulation → equation construction → algebraic transformation → discrete sequences → recursive representation**.
